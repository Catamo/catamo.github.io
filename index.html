Experience and Goals

Most of my experience comes from working with Microsoft's .NET and ASP.NET frameworks, also with .NET Core recently. I've always worked as a Full Stack Developer, from making Stored Procedures to developing complex interactions in the UI.

With the rising popularity of Front-end technologies, I've researched and learned some great tools like Vue, React, WebPack, SASS and so on, always trying to learn the best practices of each.

My goal as a professional is to be part of something that changes the world, created by me or not. I use every day of my work to enhance my foundation, and in my free time I prepare for the moment when all my potential will be needed.
